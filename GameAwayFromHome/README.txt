The Game Away from Home
Analysis of the Texas Longhorns Softball Team's performance at home and on the road

Course: SDS 375 - Sports Analytics
Tools: R, dyplr

Description: Project aims to answer if travel distance impacts the performance of softball playera and their team performance. Using data from the Teas Longhorns Softball Team, the study aims to look at average team performance and individual player performance across three seasons.
